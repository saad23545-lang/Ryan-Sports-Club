Our Story

More Than a Club —
A Legacy in Motion

Founded in 1998, RYAN Sports Club began with a single cricket ground and a vision to nurture athletic talent from every corner of Karachi. Over 26 years, we've grown into the city's most comprehensive sports facility — spanning 12 acres of world-class infrastructure.

We believe sport shapes character. Our programs welcome everyone — from toddlers discovering movement to seniors chasing personal bests — under the guidance of 45+ certified coaches.
